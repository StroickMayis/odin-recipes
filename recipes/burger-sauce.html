Ah, so you have found the secret sauce, well then...

Put this secret sauce on your burgers instead of the regular mayo and ketchup mixture. We love to dip our French fries in it too.

This stuff is exactly what you need to take your Burger, or even your Burger Dog to the next level.

Here's what you'll need:

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup chopped dill pickles
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Stir together mayonnaise, pickles, ketchup, garlic powder, and pepper in a bowl until well combined.
